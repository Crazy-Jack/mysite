About me

AI Research

Hi, I'm Tianqin Li, currently a Research Asistant and Teaching Asistant at Carnegie Mellon University, Computer Science Department and Center for the Neural Basis of Cognition, under supervision of Prof. Tai Sing Lee. I'm interested in neuro-inspired AI algorithms and its application in computer vision. Previously I also worked on contrastive representation learning with Prof. Louis-Philippe Morency in Language Technology Depertment and Prof. Ruslan Salakhutdinov in Machine Learning Department at CMU. Prior to joining CMU, I did my undergraduate in Sun Yat-sen University in GuangZhou, China. Please see my research page for more information.
